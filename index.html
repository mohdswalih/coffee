Black Coffee



No frills here: Black coffee is made from plain ground coffee beans that are brewed hot. It's served without added sugar, milk, or flavorings.



ESPRESSO



The result is a thicker, more concentrated liquid with a bolder flavor. A single espresso is a one-ounce shot.



CAPPUCCINO



This espresso-based drink is similar to a latte, but the frothy top layer is thicker.The standard ratio is equal parts espresso, steamed milk.



CORTADO



This drink, which hails from Spain, is half espresso, half steamed milk. Unlike many Italian coffee drinks, it contains little to no foam. It's typically served in a 4.5-ounce glass.



LATTE



This classic drink is typically 1/3 espresso and 2/3 steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of foam, but coffee shops have come up with seemingly endless customizations.
